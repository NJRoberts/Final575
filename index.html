Oakland Parking Meter Data

This interactive map displays parking meter revenue summarized per quarter for 2016 - 2017. Use the slider to see how meter revenue fluctuates.

The Parking Occupancy layer is based on Peak Hour occupancy rates provided by the Downtown Oakland Parking Study.

Occupancy rates are categorized by the following: Green: 0-65%, Yellow: 66-85%, Red: 85%+, and Black: No Parking / Under Construction
